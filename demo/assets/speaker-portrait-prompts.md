# Speaker Portrait Prompts

## Purpose

Use these short prompts to generate consistent website-style speaker portraits
for the three BWEC personas in ChatGPT or another image tool.

## Consistency Block

Append this consistency block to every portrait prompt:

```text
Photorealistic corporate portrait, chest-up framing, eye-level camera, soft
studio lighting, realistic skin texture, clean neutral background in muted
slate or warm grey, shallow depth of field, high-resolution website profile
photo, natural colour grading, no text, no logo, no props, no illustration,
no caricature, no exaggerated stylisation.
```

## James Whitmore

### Short Profile

British male, late 50s to early 60s, President of the British Wind Energy
Council. Experienced, composed, authoritative, and modern rather than old-
fashioned. He should read as a credible industry leader and public figure with
a subtle aristocratic polish, but never snobbish or theatrical.

### Prompt

```text
Create a photorealistic portrait of James Whitmore, a British male in his late
50s to early 60s, President of the British Wind Energy Council. He should look
experienced, intelligent, calm, and authoritative, with a modern-statesman
presence rather than a traditional establishment stereotype. Medium build,
well-groomed, silvering dark hair, clear eyes, lightly lined face, confident
but approachable expression. He is wearing a tailored dark navy suit, a pale
blue or white shirt, and a refined understated tie. The overall impression
should be credible, progressive, assured, and high-trust.
```

## Emma Collins

### Short Profile

British female, early 30s, Head of Communications and press spokesperson for
the British Wind Energy Council. Sharp, current, media-ready, and dynamic. She
should look like a modern communications lead: fast-thinking, structured,
confident, and highly presentable, with only a very subtle Scottish note in
overall character, not in costume or cliché.

### Prompt

```text
Create a photorealistic portrait of Emma Collins, a British woman in her early
30s, Head of Communications and press spokesperson for the British Wind Energy
Council. She should look modern, smart, media-ready, and confident, with a
clear professional presence and an energetic but controlled expression. She is
stylish in a contemporary business way, not glamorous or influencer-like.
Natural hair styling, sharp eyes, fresh but serious expression, tailored dark
blazer, clean blouse or knit top, polished communications-professional look.
The overall impression should be dynamic, articulate, current, and highly
credible in press and broadcast settings.
```

## Sir Alistair Pembroke

### Short Profile

British male, late 60s to early 70s, fictional senior ambassador for public
engagement and creative advocacy. Distinguished, composed, elegant, and
intellectually playful. He should look like a public intellectual with dry
charisma and quiet stage presence, not like a comedian, parody figure, or
eccentric caricature.

### Prompt

```text
Create a photorealistic portrait of Sir Alistair Pembroke, a British male in
his late 60s to early 70s, fictional Senior Ambassador for Public Engagement
and Creative Advocacy at the British Wind Energy Council. He should look
distinguished, composed, and intellectually playful, with dry charisma and a
quietly theatrical presence. Silver or white hair, expressive eyebrows, warm
but knowing expression, refined face, elegant posture. He is wearing a dark
tailored suit with subtle British character, optionally a restrained pocket
square, but nothing flamboyant. The overall impression should be senior,
memorable, witty, and high-credibility without slipping into parody.
```

## Optional Final Instruction

If you want all three images to feel like one coherent website set, add:

```text
Make this portrait part of a consistent three-person executive website series
with matching lighting, framing, background style, and photographic realism.
```
