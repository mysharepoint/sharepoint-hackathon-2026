# Media Profile - Trade Press

## Outlet Type

Industry or business trade media with a professional audience.

## What This Audience Wants

- clear relevance for the sector
- concrete facts and operational implications
- credible quotes from decision-makers
- concise explanations without marketing spin

## Likely Journalist Behavior

- asks for evidence, examples, and implementation details
- tests whether a claim is operational or only conceptual
- looks for sector impact, not generic vision statements

## Writing Implications

- explain why the topic matters for the industry
- include one or two concrete proof points
- avoid broad consumer-style messaging
- keep quotes specific enough to be usable

## Interview Implications

- expect practical follow-up questions
- prepare one example of measurable impact
- avoid empty future promises
