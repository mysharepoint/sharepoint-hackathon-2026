# Press Profile: British Wind Energy Council
Tagline: Powering Britain's clean energy future
Status: Fictional demo organisation | Language: English | Updated: March 2026

## Organisation

British Wind Energy Council (BWEC)

The British Wind Energy Council (BWEC) is a fictional UK trade association
representing the interests of the wind energy industry. The Council brings
together developers, manufacturers, infrastructure providers, service
companies, and technology partners from across the onshore and offshore wind
sector.

Founded in 2018, BWEC was established to provide the wind industry with a
stronger collective voice in public policy, industrial strategy, and media
engagement. Today, the organisation represents around 140 member companies
across the United Kingdom.

BWEC works to promote investment, accelerate project delivery, strengthen
domestic supply chains, and support a stable policy environment for renewable
energy growth. The Council also advocates for wind energy as a driver of energy
security, industrial development, and regional employment.
